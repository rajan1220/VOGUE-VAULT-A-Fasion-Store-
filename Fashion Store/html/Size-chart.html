How to Measure Your Foot

Stand on a piece of paper and mark the longest part of your foot (heel to toe). Measure this distance in inches to find your size.

How to Measure Yourself

  • Chest/Bust

    Measure around the fullest part of your chest/bust, keeping the tape measure parallel to the floor and under your arms.

  • Waist

    Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist, typically just above your belly button.

  • Hips

    Stand with feet together and measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.

  • Inseam

    For pants, measure from the crotch to the bottom of your ankle along the inside of your leg.
