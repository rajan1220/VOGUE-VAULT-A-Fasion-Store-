Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for store credit or exchange. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached.

Exchange Policy

Exchange any item within 45 days for a different size or color. Exchanges are subject to availability of the requested item.

Refund Policy

Refunds are issued to the original payment method for eligible returns. Processing may take 5-10 business days after we receive your return.

Learn more

How to Return an Item

  • Initiate Your Return

    Log in to your account and go to Order History to start your return. You'll receive a prepaid return label via email.

  • Package Your Items

    Place items in their original packaging with all tags attached. Include the original packing slip or invoice.

  • Ship Your Return

    Attach the provided return label and drop off at any UPS location. You'll receive an email when we process your return.

  • Receive Your Refund

    Once received, we'll inspect your items and process your refund within 3-5 business days.
